What i do

I have a strong background in designing, implementing, and maintaining IT and network environments for small, medium, and large companies. I have gained extensive knowledge in administering monitoring and security systems, including automating processes, both on premises and in the cloud. I possess the necessary skills to set up servers for monitoring and filtering network traffic, utilize tools for penetration testing and detecting backdoors, and implement applications for network sniffing and analysis. Additionally, I have experience in implementing firewalls and UTM platforms with Sophos, Check Point, and Fortinet, including configuring rules for web proxy (both explicit and transparent), integrating authentication via Kerberos and NTLM on virtual schemas based on Microsoft systems over Hyper-V, configuring IPS and antivirus controls, and implementing reversing proxy with SSL Portals.

My experience has taught me the importance of teamwork, effective communication, and leadership. I take a proactive approach to identifying issues and providing strategic solutions. I am committed to continuous learning and improvement, and I am capable of adapting to changing scenarios.

Specialties: management, cloud, containers, leadership, strategy, writing, IT and network architecture, virtualization, serverless, Cisco, Mikrotik, Fortinet, Sophos, OpenStack, Google Cloud, AWS, Azure, Kubernetes, Docker, Linux, Ansible, Git, DevOps, CI/CD, Azure DevOps, speaking, lecturing, writing, and certification.

In my current role, I work on:

  • Constantly improving existing networks to maximize efficiency and security.
  • Developing and implementing new deployment and scaling processes.
  • Participating in the creation of automation tools and managing and organizing relevant documents and records.
  • Standardizing and generating processes for the implementation of staging and development environments in Microsoft Azure with CI/CD using GitHub Actions and Azure DevOps.
  • Implementing Azure AD, as well as configuring and managing the Microsoft 365 suite, including SharePoint and advanced security tools such as Microsoft Intune and Microsoft 365 Defender.
  • Conducting ongoing analysis, implementation, and monitoring of information security best practices.
